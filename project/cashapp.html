Project overview

Cashapp is a revolutionary mobile payment platform that simplifies peer-to-peer money transfers and digital banking. Our design approach focused on creating an intuitive, secure, and engaging user experience that makes financial transactions feel effortless and trustworthy.

The challenge was to design a financial app that appeals to a younger demographic while maintaining the security and reliability expected from banking applications. We achieved this through bold visual design, micro-interactions, and a simplified user flow.

Key Features

  • Instant money transfers between friends and family
  • Integrated debit card for seamless spending
  • Bitcoin trading capabilities
  • Cash Boost rewards program
  • Direct deposit functionality

Design Philosophy

We created a design system that breaks away from traditional banking aesthetics. Using vibrant green as the primary brand color, playful animations, and clear typography, the app feels more like a social platform than a banking tool.
