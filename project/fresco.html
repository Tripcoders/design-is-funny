Project overview

What is Fresco? imagine Apple CarPlay but for recipes and smart cooking appliances in the Kitchen

We helped them shape and re design the whole experience of the product, from the “Guided Cooking” to “My Kitchen” solving complex problems and elevating the design with “Fresco” moments

The Design

We re-invented and re-designed the whole experience for Fresco, from Onboarding, to the Discovery Feed, and the core "Fresco moments" like "Guided Cooking" and the whole "My Kitchen" enviroment that lives within the app and scales on multiple screens, from the mobile app to the actual touch screen of the physical appliance

We designed a whole scalable system for Fresco, allowing the users to control the appliances directly from the app, being able to adjust and change settings, and also track the whole cooking process with the help of interactive "pills" to make sure that everythings is running smoothly in the cooking process.
